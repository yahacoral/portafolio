WHERE I'VE BEEN

When I graduated from Pontifical Catholic University of Peru in 2018 with a degree in Publicity I was confident in pursuing my career as a publicist & marketer. After I worked in a global technology consultancy, everything shifted, as I started to relate with programmers and ux designers who told me about their jobs and the endless possibilities that the world of technology offers you.

At that point in my life, I decided to become a full-time freelancer to study programming. Happily, I had the support of my boyfriend who dedicated himself to teaching me and bringing me tools for my learning. I joined intensive web development programs and after my first lesson I knew that becoming a developer was what I had to do with my life.

WHAT I'M DOING

I'm a front-end developer and designer based in Lima - Perú. My specialty is full website strategy, design and development, making pixel magic in photoshop and turning it into beautiful, semantic HTML & CSS. Also, I enjoy build both small and large scale applications in JavaScript for desktop, tablet or mobile.

My interests, however, extend beyond the web and I also love helping people with branding and marketing strategy.

When I'm not designing, I'm probably having fun with my boyfriend, watching a movie, making a new recipe or in a karaoke session at home.

WHAT I HOPE TO DO

My main purpose is to get better every day. Personally and Professionally. Every day is a chance at improvement. For instance, these days I am really into in UX design.
